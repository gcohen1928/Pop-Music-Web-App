Restard Game and Show Instructions

Welcome to the Billboard 100 Challenge!


How to Play:

1. Press the play button on the buttom of your screen

2. 3 cards will appear with 3 random songs from the Billboard 100's list

3. Your goal is to guess which song is the most popular, least popular, and in between out of the three songs

4. When you think you have an answer type (ONLY type the number in quotes):

- "1" for the most popular

- "2" for middle popular

- "3" for least popular

5. After you typed your answers, press the submit button

6. The correct order will be shown, and you can play another round by pressing the Play Again button

7. Keep track of your progress on the scoreboard at the button of your screen

8. Have fun!
